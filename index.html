How It Works

CheckPoint uses cutting-edge language models and verified data sources to detect bias, trace claims to their origins, and connect you to credible fact-checkers — seamlessly integrated within your browser experience.

1. Scan

Highlight a sentence or headline, and CheckPoint instantly analyzes it for bias, tone, and linguistic framing.

2. Trace

Discover where claims originate using real-time web indices, media graph mapping, and source clustering.

3. Verify

Get quick references from IFCN-approved fact-checkers and trusted outlets to confirm accuracy or expose misinformation.

About CheckPoint

CheckPoint is developed by students from UC Berkeley and UCLA — Love Adu, Dev Raja, Tenisha Morohan, and Morwena Trinh — combining expertise in media, policy, computer science, and data ethics. Our goal is to empower users to navigate digital information with confidence and clarity.
